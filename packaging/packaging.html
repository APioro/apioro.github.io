The Suits Series: A Tarot-Themed Cosmetic Line
PROJECT GOAL

This self-initiated project reimagines cosmetic packaging through the lens of tarot symbolism. The goal was to build a distinctive, whimsical brand from scratch—one that stands out on the shelf, feels immersive, and remains consistent across multiple product lines (suits) like Wands and Cups. The system was designed to scale, with room to expand into additional suits - Swords and Pentacles.
DESIGN SOLUTIONS

Each package features a custon vector illustration that combines the tarot suit symbol (Cup or Wand) with the product’s key botanical ingredient. In the background, flowing or flame-like shapes subtly represent each suit’s elemental theme—Water for Cups, Fire for Wands.
A rich, dark color palette evokes a mystical, mysterious feel. Background symmetry mirrors the structure of tarot cards to reinforce the theme. Typography complements the illustration style and ensures clarity across formats.
